Educational Background

I studied senior high school at...

CIIT Senior High School Inc.

CIIT SHS was very culture-shocking—environment, teachers, events, and more. It was very different from where I grew up with and interacted with. It offered me the space and freedom I have been hoping to have.

The first time I saw the school, I immediately loved it and wished to be a part of their community. Aside from the advantage of learning the specialized program I wanted, it felt like I could break free from my shackles if I choose this school. It wasn't well-received, of course. My personality of weighing the pros and cons and considering the opinions of others made me think twice.

In the end, my heart won and I pursued CIIT. It was the best decision I ever made.
